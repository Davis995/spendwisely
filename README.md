# SpendWise - Your Financial Wellness Companion

SpendWise is a gamified personal finance app designed for Uganda that helps users track expenses in Ugandan Shillings (UGX), build better spending habits, and achieve their savings goals through positive reinforcement and gentle guidance.

## ğŸŒŸ Features

### Core Functionality
- **Expense Tracking**: Quick and easy expense logging with categorization
- **Budget Management**: Set monthly budgets and track spending in real-time
- **Gamification**: Earn points for logging expenses and staying under budget
- **Progress Visualization**: Beautiful charts and progress bars to track your journey

### Gamification Elements
- **Points System**: 
  - +1 point for each logged expense
  - +5 points for staying under daily budget
  - +20 points for reaching savings goals
- **Badges & Achievements**: Unlock rewards for consistent behavior
- **Challenges**: Weekly and daily challenges to keep you motivated
- **Leaderboards**: Friendly competition with other users

### Design Philosophy
- **Warm Color Palette**: Calming beige, cream, and brown tones
- **Positive Psychology**: Encouragement over criticism
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for daily use on mobile devices
- **Minimal & Clean**: Focus on what matters most

### Uganda Localization
- **Currency**: All amounts displayed in Ugandan Shillings (UGX)
- **Local Context**: Expense suggestions include local items like boda boda, matatu, rolex (chapati)
- **Appropriate Amounts**: Budget examples and challenges use realistic UGX amounts
- **Cultural Relevance**: Payment methods and services relevant to Uganda
